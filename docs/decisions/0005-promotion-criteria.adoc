# 5. Promotion criteria: when spline stops being a seed

Date: 2026-07-09

## Status

Accepted

Answers seed question 5 (`docs/SEED-QUESTIONS.adoc`).

## Context

spline was spun off at owner direction but kept deliberately thin, so that the
revert cost stays low while the squisher-shaped questions were answered. ADRs
0002–0004 answer them on paper. Paper is not promotion: "wire first — unwired
is not done." This ADR fixes the bar, so that neither enthusiasm nor drift can
move it later.

## Decision

spline leaves seed/thin status **ONLY when ALL four** of the following hold:

a. **burble control-plane traffic runs over its Bebop codecs end-to-end in live
   transport.** Not generated, not unit-tested — live. (Today JSON is on the
   wire and the codecs are dormant; see ADR 0003.)
b. **A second consumer (gossamer) parses the same plane.** One consumer proves
   a private format; two prove an alignment.
c. **The alignment mapping of ADR 0003 has a conformance test in groove CI.**
   The mapping stops being prose and starts being checked.
d. **The typed-token boundary of ADR 0004 is exercised by the gossamer↔burble
   pairing demo.** Opaque string in, linear handle at each endpoint, posture
   absent from bytes (TS-7).

Until all four hold: **no `src/` in spline.** Docs, ADRs, and conformance
fixtures only. Revert cost stays low (delete a dir, or fold the docs back into
groove).

### What "stable" would additionally need

Promotion out of seed is not stability. "Stable" additionally requires:

* a **versioned mapping** — the ADR 0003 alignment mapping carries a version,
  with a recorded compatibility rule for evolving it;
* **two independent plane implementations** — two implementations of the same
  plane's framing that pass the same conformance fixtures, so the contract is
  demonstrably the document, not one codebase's behaviour.

## Consequences

### Positive

- The bar is written down and enumerable. A status doc can say "2 of 4" and be
  checked.
- The first milestone is concrete and small: wire burble's existing, tested
  codecs into its live transport (criterion (a)) — work that happens in burble,
  which is editable (ADR 0003).
- No premature `src/` means no premature API to regret.

### Negative

- Criterion (b) and (d) depend on gossamer work that has its own timeline;
  spline can be blocked by a consumer. Accepted: an alignment layer with one
  consumer is not yet an alignment layer.

### Neutral

- These criteria measure promotion of *spline*; they do not gate burble or
  gossamer releases.
